Our Journey

Lenergy Logistics is based in Kenya; a Transport, Logistics and Communications firm that serves a wide geographical area in the region.

Lenergy Logistics was founded in 2017 and has cultivated "end-to-end" solutions for the ever-changing industry culture of shifting trends.

We align our strategic capabilities and competitive advantages: knowledge, experience and resources.

With reputation and proficiency that interplays solely on transparency, operational excellence and holistic concepts of different services we offer:

Overland Transport & Logistics

Lenergy Logistics tailored transport, begins by listening to you – on the basis of your needs.

With transport modalities that guarantee time-definite shipment’s delivery from point of source to final destination day in, day out, all-year round.

Our expertise in over-the-road transport allows seamless transport flexibility, advisory services, designs internal process flows, identify gaps and translate options that reflect rigorous operations analysis.

We minimize logistics costs, increase traceability throughout the supply chain and improve productivity of resources.


General Goods & Supplies

Lenergy Logistics has a wide range of product supplies from enterprises, system integrators, repairs and service companies.

We offer an unrivalled service meeting the needs of our customers by sourcing and supplying products to the highest standards, in addition to the special prices we offer and accountability.

For sustainability, we deploy controls and transformations on how goods are supplied.


Disaster Management & Emergency Relief

Lenergy Logistics has strategic framework to natural and man-made adverse events with an integrated disaster management direction and coordination of risk assessment and reduction.

We assist to rebuild, empower and work in building recovery shelter, reconstruction kits, household supplies, safe water and sanitation with continuous crisis preparedness.

Whatever the challenge – our work is always implementation driven.


Unified Communications (UC)

Lenergy Logistics unification tightly links unprecedented levels of efficient communication experience any time, in any place and on any device from anyone to anywhere.

The (UC) brings collaborative applications together for field and remote operators, lowers communications costs, makes organizations more agile and elevates productivity to new heights.

The collaborative tools include: Video, IP Telephony and Online meetings (or web conferencing) with Real-time team to assist Civic Education.

Our Principles

We conduct our business affairs in the best interests of the Company and avoid situations where our private interests interfere in any way with our Company’s interests.

A “conflict of interest” can occur when employees’ personal interest interferes in any way with or may appear to interfere in any way with the interests of the Company as a whole.

Our Commitment

We strive to deliver solutions that adhere to professionalism, integrity and achieving excellence in our service to our customers.

We stand by our customers and helping them pursue the best solutions for their business with trusting, lasting and prosperous relationships for both parties, achieving sustained growth economically, socially and environmentally.
